HQ-1 OSPF Address Family
------------------------
router ospfv3 1
 router-id 1.1.1.1
 
 address-family ipv4 unicast
  passive-interface FastEthernet0/0
 exit-address-family
 
 address-family ipv6 unicast
  passive-interface FastEthernet0/0
 exit-address-family

inter fa 1/1
 ospfv3 1 ipv6 area 0
 ospfv3 1 ipv4 area 0
 ospfv3 1 hello-interval 60
 ospfv3 1 dead-interval 180

inter ser 2/1
 ospfv3 1 ipv6 area 1
 ospfv3 1 ipv4 area 1
 ospfv3 1 hello-interval 60
 ospfv3 1 dead-interval 180

inter fa 0/0
 ospfv3 1 ipv6 area 0
 ospfv3 1 ipv4 area 0
